TRIBUTE TO PHOEBE MUGA ASIYO

Who she was

She was the first woman to the position of Luo elder for her efforts to promote education for girls, women's rights and gender equality in Kenya.

Why I admire her

She was determined and courageous to dedicated her life to improving the political arena in Kenya, the role of women and girls and those affected by the HIV epidemic.

Her Achievements

  • She was the first woman in Kenya with it's 42 communities to become an elder.
  • Order of the Golden worrior: 1st class chief of the burning spear
  • Doctor of Humane Letter from Lehigh University
  • Honorary Doctorate Law Degree from the University of York (2002)
